## Rising Star from Australia

Jett Lawrence is one of the most exciting talents in the sport of motocross and supercross. Born and raised in Landsborough, Queensland, Australia, Jett and his brother Hunter moved to Europe at a young age to pursue their racing dreams before eventually coming to America.

Since arriving in the United States, Jett has quickly established himself as one of the most talented and charismatic riders in the paddock. His smooth riding style, combined with his aggressive race craft, has made him a fan favorite and a championship contender.

## Professional Career

Jett turned pro in 2019 at the age of 16 and immediately showed promise in the 250 class. In 2021, he clinched his first Pro Motocross 250 championship, followed by another 250 championship in 2022.

The 2023 season marked his transition to the premier 450 class in outdoor motocross, where he made history by becoming the first rookie to win every single round of the Pro Motocross Championship, achieving a perfect season. He followed this by winning the inaugural SuperMotocross World Championship.

In 2024, Jett made his 450 Supercross debut and quickly established himself as a championship contender, winning multiple rounds in his rookie season.

## Riding Style

Jett is known for his incredibly smooth riding style that makes even the most technical sections look effortless. He excels in all conditions but is particularly strong in ruts and technical sections where his precision and bike control set him apart.

His starts have improved dramatically over the years, and he's known for his ability to make aggressive passes when needed while maintaining clean racing.

## Off the Track

Off the track, Jett is known for his playful personality and close relationship with his brother Hunter, who also races in the 450 class for Team Honda HRC. The brothers are known for their entertaining social media presence and approachable demeanor with fans.

Jett has become one of the most marketable athletes in the sport, with numerous sponsorships beyond his factory Honda deal. His popularity continues to grow as he accumulates more championships and showcases his talent on the world stage.
