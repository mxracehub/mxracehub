Factory Teams
#

Factory teams are directly supported by motorcycle manufacturers and represent the highest level of the sport with the best resources, equipment, and technical support.

Team Honda HRC

American Honda's factory team with Jett Lawrence, Hunter Lawrence, and Chance Hymas.

Manufacturer: Honda Founded: 1973

Red Bull KTM Factory Racing

KTM's factory effort with Chase Sexton, Aaron Plessinger, and Tom Vialle.

Manufacturer: KTM Founded: 2000

Monster Energy Yamaha Star Racing

Yamaha's premier team with Cooper Webb, Justin Cooper, and Haiden Deegan.

Manufacturer: Yamaha Founded: 1998

Monster Energy Kawasaki

Kawasaki's factory racing team with Jason Anderson and Adam Cianciarulo.

Manufacturer: Kawasaki Founded: 1979

Rockstar Energy Husqvarna Factory Racing

Husqvarna's factory team with Malcolm Stewart, Christian Craig, and RJ Hampshire.

Manufacturer: Husqvarna Founded: 2014

Red Bull GASGAS Factory Racing

GASGAS factory effort with Justin Barcia and Pierce Brown.

Manufacturer: GASGAS Founded: 2020

Support Teams
#

Support teams receive varying levels of factory assistance and represent an important tier in the sport’s competitive structure.

Monster Energy Pro Circuit Kawasaki

Legendary 250 class team with Levi Kitchen, Max Anstie, and Cameron McAdoo.

Manufacturer: Kawasaki Founded: 1991

Muc-Off FXR ClubMX Yamaha

Yamaha-supported team with Phil Nicoletti, Garrett Marchbanks, and Enzo Lopes.

Manufacturer: Yamaha Founded: 2012

Fire Power Honda

Honda-supported team with Dean Wilson and Max Anstie.

Manufacturer: Honda Founded: 2019

Troy Lee Designs Red Bull GASGAS

Official GASGAS support team with Pierce Brown and Michael Mosiman.

Manufacturer: GASGAS Founded: 2004

H.E.P. Motorsports Suzuki

Suzuki's main team with Ken Roczen and Shane McElrath.

Manufacturer: Suzuki Founded: 2018
